Introduction

Here you will find the latest information about the AOD Battlefront Division, as well as what's going on ingame. We hope to bring you a variety of news to keep you informed and up to date with the sweet stuff happening throughout the month.

Latest News

Promotions: Congratulations to everyone that was promoted this month and thank you to the NCO's for working so hard in bringing in great players to Battlefront and making this division a great success!

Training: As we all know we all have something that we want to improve in our play style. Whether that is Sniping skills, Map layout, or maybe some Air Combat Training; we have a session that fits your needs. The training program is completely open to anyone that wants to join.

Events: Looking for great ways to get involved in team play with fellow AOD members? Look no further! Each week there are cool opportunities to play in events like Tactical Tuesday, Wookie Wednesday, or Super Hero Saturday. If you do not find an event that tickles your fancy, please let us know and we can host an event that you are interested in seeing.

May The 4th Be With Us - CO Invitational

May the 4th is an important day in the lives of Star Wars fans, and nothing could commemorate it more than an event to fit the occasion. Our leader and CO, CupOHemlock hosted an Invitational that garnered more than 36 people throughout the day in playing Walker Assault, Blast and DLC matches and the first ever coveted Battlefront CO Invitational Gold and Silver badges, which were awarded to each team based on who won the event. I am proud to say that the Youth team beat the Experience team in true AOD fashion! It was a BLAST and pleasure to see such a great turnout for the CO's Invitational event!
